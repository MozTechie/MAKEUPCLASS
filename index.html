About

What is MozGlam

MozGlam was founded with the goal of empowering individuals through the art of makeup.
We believe that everyone has the potential to become a makeup expert with the right training
and commitment. With a team of experienced instructors and a tailored curriculum, MozGlam
offers classes for all levels, from beginners to professionals. Each of our sessions is designed to
help you understand makeup techniques, the latest trends, and the best products on the market.
Join our community and discover new ways to express yourself through beauty.
